fallout dialog graphs : ru-f2-mods

Some graphs are large and may be off the screen initially. The best way to explore them is either by dragging with mouse and zooming in/out by control-mouse-wheel-up/down or by keyboard arrows and zooming in/out with control-plus/control-minus/control-zero. Modern browsers are supported (seemingly).
